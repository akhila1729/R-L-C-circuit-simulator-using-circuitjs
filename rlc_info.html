R-L-C circuit simulator

The R-L-C circuit simulator solves R-L-C circuit consisting of resistor,inductor and capacitor and plots graph of current vs time and voltage vs time. This is very useful for R-L-C circuit analysis.R-L-C circuit have many applications as oscillatory circuits snd is also used for tuning purposes. It is also used as band filter,low-pass filter or high-pass filter.Resistor,Inductor and Capacitor can be connected either in series or parallel.
